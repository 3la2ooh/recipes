Ingredients:
 - 1 tomatoe
 - 1 onion
 - 6 eggs
 - salt
 - pepper
 - tumieric
 - ground red pepper
 - butter 

Directions:
  1. Cut tomatoes and onions into small pieces
  2. In a medium saucepan, melt butter and add onions
  3. When onions become yellow-ish, add tomatoes and Stir.
  4. After 2 minutes add the salt, pepper, tumieric and ground red pepper.
  5. Add the eggs and cook to your own pleasure.
